Kentucky's National Register listed Civil War Sites

This map presents some of Kentucky's National Register listed Civil War sites. The locations are in the form of points and polygons. The points represent buildings, objects, sites, and structures, while the polygons represent buildings, districts, sites, and structures.

To use this map of Kentucky's National Register listed Civil War sites, navigate to and zoom in on the map above in any particular area to see what sites may be present. Alternatively, use this full-scale map.

Please be aware that the dataset may contain spatial errors. For more information, see the "Can you explain why a feature looks ‘odd’ in the GIS dataset?" explanation found in the FAQ for the datasaet.

Data used

The data was extracted from the National Register of Historic Places, geospatial database as of August 2024. To explore the full database, please visit the National Register of Historic Places webmap.

Tools used in this project

  • QGIS version 3.38.3 was used to extract the data from a ESRI ArcGIS geodatabase.
  • QGIS version 3.38.3 was used to export the extrated data to a JSON file, an open format for text representation of vector spatial data.
  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled.

The map does not include any sites deemed "restricted" or "sensitive", such as archaeological sites.
